Browser support:
- desktop: 
  - Chrome, Firefox, Opera - supported;
  - Safari, Edge - in development;
  - IE - no support;
- mobile:
  - android:
    - Chrome, Samsung Internet - supported;
  - iOS:
    - Safari, Chrome (thin wrapper around re-skinned Safari) - in development;

See https://caniuse.com/#search=service%20worker and https://jakearchibald.github.io/isserviceworkerready/
